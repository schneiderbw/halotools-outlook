Outlook task-pane add-in

Surface the sender's Halo contact and open tickets next to the email. Append the email to a ticket or create a new one without leaving Outlook. RFC-standard threading via Message-ID so replies attach to the right ticket automatically.

Compose side

Templates, KB inserts, log-on-send

On the compose surface: insert your Halo canned text or KB articles, drop a ticket link, and toggle "log this email to a ticket when I send." A launch-event handler runs on Send and appends the email to the ticket you picked — no second tab.

  • RFC Message-ID / In-Reply-To threading — no Halo custom fields needed
  • Inline ticket actions (status, priority, agent, due date)
  • Insert Halo canned text + create new canned texts from inside Outlook
  • Activity timeline via the Halo /Feed endpoint
  • "Create contact in Halo" for unmatched senders
  • Self-stamping: the plug-in fills its own threading fields
  • Per-MSP packaging — your tenant's URL + Client ID baked in
  • Pure SPA — no backend, no token storage on our side
